Autonomy

Smugglers operate outside the control of the Padishah Emperor, the Guild, or the Great Houses. Set your own schedules, choose your missions, and reap higher rewards than working for established powers.

Ventures

A smuggler's life on Arrakis is full of danger and excitement. Encounter sandworms, rival smugglers, and potentially Fremen warriors.

Rare Goods

Smugglers have the opportunity to obtain and trade rare or illegal goods that are not readily available through official channels. This could include anything from advanced technology to forbidden luxuries.
